Described as a hacker, thinker, and an artist, cipherboy [a.k.a. Alexander Scheel] strives to bridge the gap between security and ease of use. While his interest in cryptography started at age 10, passion and joy fill his play, practice, and work. When he is not studying or coding, you will often find him sequestered in a practice room perfecting Czajkowski or Bach. As he gets the time, he will study and optimize security, mathematics, and cryptography. He is currently looking for technical internships for May 2017.
