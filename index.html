Alexander Scheel is a software developer at Red Hat working on Red Hat Certificate System. He is the current maintainer of JSS, a NSS wrapper for Java. He is a strong advocate for Open Source and Open Access research. Alex contributes to various projects on GitHub and to the Fedora Package Ecosystem (including the Stewardship SIG) under the nickname cipherboy. In his free time, he studies violin and enjoys taking photographs of the places he goes.
